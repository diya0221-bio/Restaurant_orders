# Restaurant_orders
Analyzed orders and menu data to uncover top-selling items, peak ordering hours, revenue by category, and average order value. Insights highlight customer favorites, busy times, and revenue trends for better business decisions.


--Data Cleaning

Removed duplicates and handled missing values.

Merged menu and orders datasets for analysis.


--Analysis Findings

-Top 5 Ordered Items:

Hamburger (622), Edamame (620), Korean Beef Bowl (588), Cheeseburger (583), French Fries (571)

Indicates strong preference for classic fast-food and quick meals.

-Total Revenue:

₹159,217.9, showing strong overall sales.

-Revenue by Category:

Italian (₹49,462.7) and Asian (₹46,720.65) lead revenue.

Followed by Mexican (₹34,796.8) and American (₹28,237.75).

Customers favor Italian and Asian cuisine.

-Peak Ordering Hours:

12 PM–1 PM is the busiest (lunchtime).

Smaller peak 5–6 PM (dinner time).

-Average Order Value (AOV):

Around ₹30 per order, with slight daily fluctuations, showing consistent customer spending.

This analysis gives a clear picture of customer preferences, peak hours, and revenue distribution across categories, useful for menu planning and marketing strategies.
